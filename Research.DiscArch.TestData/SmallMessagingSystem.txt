R1. Under high system load, ensuring message delivery is paramount, even if it exceeds the usual delivery timeframe.
R2. Under typical conditions, messages should be delivered within a few seconds.
R3. In cases of slow connectivity, the system should make multiple delivery attempts to guarantee message receipt.
R4. Messages can contain up to 1,000 characters.
R5. The system should dynamically reduce resource usage to lower costs when there are fewer active users.
R6. The system should autonomously implement necessary updates during periods of low activity.
R7. Users must have the capability to delete their messages even after delivery.
